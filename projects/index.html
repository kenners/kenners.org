Most of my work is published on my GitHub profile. Here's some of the highlights.

LA Calc

LA Calc is an iOS app designed to assist medical professionals calculate safe doses of common local anaesthetic agents for local infiltration. It also provides reference doses for common agents and an algorithm for the immediate management of local anaesthetic toxicity.

Intended for use by qualified medical professionals only.

Uamuzi Bora

Uamuzi Bora is a UK charity who use mobile information technology and an electronic medical record to improve public health and primary care in Kenya. I co-founded the project with John Haskew in 2009, and was the lead developer of the electronic medical records system for four years.
