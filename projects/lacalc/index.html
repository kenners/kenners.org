LA Calc is an app designed to assist medical professionals calculate safe doses of common local anaesthetic agents for local infiltration.

It also provides reference doses for common agents and an algorithm for the immediate management of local anaesthetic toxicity.

Intended for use by qualified medical professionals only.

Available for both iOS and Android.
