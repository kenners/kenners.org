Possum calculates the Portsmouth Physiological and Operative Severity Score for the enUmeration of Mortality and morbidity (P-POSSUM) score for general surgical patients, using the algorithm described by Prytherch et al.

Intended for use by qualified medical professionals only.

Available for iOS and Android.
